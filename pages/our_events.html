Our Events

Solidworks Basics

SolidWorks is a solid modeling computer-aided design and computer-aided engineering computer program. The Solidworks program considers today as one of the leaders' programs in our days. The workshops made them know how to make their drawings and how to use mechanical tools.

HVAC

This course introduced the air conditioning systems. The course explained from the very beginning calculations, types of heat, etc. to how to choose and simulate a fully air conditioning system.

Fresh Co. for cookers field trip

This field trip was introduced to the students to teach them specified things. We want to teach them how to interact in factory, learn manufacturing, types of pumps and types of Sheetmetal forming. We choose Fresh company as one of the leaders at home appliances field.

Courses Solidworks Simulation

The most interesting about a body after drawing and assembly is to see the efficiency of that body. By the course we introduced, we abled the students to enter the field of simulation analysis not only the Solidworks simulation. We gave them an overview about the system analysis in most of the programs nowadays.

Arduino and proteus

This course was introduced to help the students in the controlling field. As a mechanical and mechatronics engineering student activity, we see it was best entrance to the control field by learning C++ programming language which is the most important programing language nowadays.
